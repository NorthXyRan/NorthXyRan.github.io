# Miscellaneous

I am also a pianist. I have been playing since the age of three. Alongside my solo work, I remain actively engaged as an accompanist in university settings, including conducting classes and graduation concerts. I am an avid lover of both chamber music and concertos.

In recent years, my artistic focus has centered on the Romantic repertoire, particularly the Ballades, Nocturnes, and Concertos of Chopin. In addition, I perform selected sonatas from across the Classical and Romantic traditions, including Mozart’s K.331, Beethoven’s Op. 90 and Schubert’s D.960, all of which hold particular personal significance in my repertoire.

I stay active through basketball, tennis, swimming, and fitness. I'm also an amateur photographer who enjoys film cameras and drones.
